My preferred role

Based on how you wish to interact with the package, you might take on one of the following roles.

User

As a user of the package, you may wish to minimize the start-up time, i.e., the duration between submitting your calculation and recieving an output. This is often referred to in the Julia community as "time to first X" (TTFX). PackageCompiler.jl allows you to create a custom system image by precompiling specific dependencies in order to reduce start-up time.
